100 g parlsey
50 g butter
2 onions chopped
2 potatoes chopped
1.2 liters chicken stock
salt pepper

150 ml light cream
chopped parsley

cut stalks off parsley, fry onoin and potato for 5 mins, add stock and stalks, simmer till potatoes are done, let cool a bit, remove stalks, and add parsley, and puree, salt and pepper.
