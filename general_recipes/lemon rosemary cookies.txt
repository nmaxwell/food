 Lemon Rosemary Cookies

1 cup sugar
1 cup butter
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1 teaspoon chopped rosemary
2 eggs
3 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon lemon zest

mix 1 cup sugar, butter, 1 tablespoon zest, rosemary, and eggs in a bowl. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt. shape dough into roll 12 inches long and wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours. 

Heat oven to 375. mix 3 tablespoons of sugar and 1 teaspoon zest. roll dough in suga mixture to coat. Cut into slices and place on cookie sheet. Bake 10 to 12 minutes.
